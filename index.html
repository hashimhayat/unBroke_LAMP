Unbroke

UnBroke is a platform where an array of services or can be looked up. It aims to connect the people who are looking for a job with those who need to get a job done. For example if a person A is looking for a mathematics tutor for his son, a user on the application who can teach maths can be connected to the person who is looking for a person to do the job for him. Hence, it aims to provides an easy way of making money while honing the skills of individuals. This application provides the working-class with a stress-free way of finding people to complete a respective job.
